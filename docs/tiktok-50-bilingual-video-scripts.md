Purpose: 50 short-form video scripts for TikTok/Reels/Shorts. Each video is designed for about 20 seconds with a digital-human presenter plus screen-recording or dashboard-style b-roll.

Ground rules:
- Do not claim GoCNScout guarantees supplier safety, compliance, or factory status.
- Do not claim the free scan verifies certificates, performs factory audits, bypasses Alibaba login/CAPTCHA, or unlocks private contacts.
- Do not show real company names, phone numbers, personal emails, private addresses, or real supplier screenshots unless permitted.
- Use GoCNScout as a public-source screening, supplier database, report, shortlist, manual review, and data-license product.

Recommended presenter persona:
- Calm supply-chain risk analyst, 30-40 years old, navy blazer, professional but not stiff.
- Tone: "slow down before you pay", not "I found a secret hack".
- Visual rhythm: digital human hook, product screen/b-roll middle, CTA end card.
