# gocnscout Pricing

All prices are in USD.

gocnscout provides supplier database subscriptions, industry PDF reports, custom shortlist requests, and annual data licensing. All public exports and reports exclude private personal contact data.

## Free

Price: $0/month

Limits:
- Search results: 10 results per page
- Search access: up to 2 pages
- Supplier profile views: 5 per month
- CSV export: not included
- Website links: not included
- Saved lists: not included
- Team seats: not included

Included:
- Public SEO pages
- Basic keyword search
- Industry filters
- Province and city filters
- Limited public supplier profile preview

Limits:
- Search results: 25 results per page
- Supplier profile views: unlimited
- CSV export: 200 supplier rows per month
- Saved lists: 5
- Saved suppliers: 200
- Supplier comparison: up to 5 suppliers
- Included report downloads: 1 per month
- Team seats: 1

Included:
- Basic filters
- Advanced filters
- Official website links when available
- Email support

## Industry PDF Reports

Basic report: $49/report
Full report: $99/report
Premium report: $299/report
China sourcing intelligence report: $149/report

Reports are uploaded and published by the admin team. Reports contain industry analysis, supplier counts, distribution summaries, sample public profiles, and buyer verification checklists. Reports do not include private personal contact data.

Price: $399/request

Delivery:

- Company profile summary

- Buyer verification checklist

Not included:
- Private contact names
- Mobile numbers
- Personal emails
- Phone marketing lists
