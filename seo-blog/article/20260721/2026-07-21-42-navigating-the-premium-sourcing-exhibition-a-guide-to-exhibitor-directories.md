> Pro Tip / Warning: Before committing to any payment or large order with a new exhibitor, ensure you verify these 5 critical points:
  
    Business License Verification: Confirm the company's legal registration and operational status in China.
    Export License & Customs Records: Verify their actual export history and product categories through official customs data.
    Production Capacity & Quality Certifications: Request ISO 9001, CE, or other relevant certifications and audit reports (e.g., BSCI, Sedex).
    Bank Account Consistency: Ensure the payment beneficiary name matches the registered company name on their business license.
    Physical Factory Audit: If possible, arrange a third-party audit of their production facility to confirm manufacturing capabilities, not just trading.
