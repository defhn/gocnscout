## Frequently Asked Questions

### How to share CAD engineering drawings with Chinese factories safely?

Sharing sensitive intellectual property like CAD drawings requires robust protection. We strongly recommend implementing a comprehensive Non-Disclosure, Non-Circumvention, and Non-Use (NNN) agreement, drafted under Chinese law, before sharing any proprietary information. Additionally, use secure, encrypted file transfer protocols and consider watermarking your drawings. For further guidance on IP protection in international trade, refer to resources from the [World Trade Organization (WTO)](https://www.wto.org/).

### What is the typical lead time for custom molded samples?

The typical lead time for custom molded samples can vary significantly based on complexity, material availability, and factory workload. Generally, for new tooling and first article inspection (FAI), expect anywhere from 4 to 8 weeks in 2026. This period includes mold design, tool fabrication, initial sample production, and quality checks. Subsequent revisions or material changes can extend this timeline. Always clarify the exact lead time with your chosen **top custom manufacturer in China** and factor in potential delays.
